Hardware

Smart Field Device

Purpose-built IoT hardware designed for Nepal's field conditions. Collects environmental data and transmits it to our cloud API in real time.

ESP32-S3 Powered

Solar-powered field unit with 4 integrated sensors — sends data directly to AgroPan's web API

ESP32-S3 Microcontroller

Dual-core processor with built-in WiFi. Handles sensor acquisition, local processing, and secure API transmission with ultra-low power.

Soil Moisture Sensor

Capacitive sensor measures volumetric water content. Data is logged to the cloud so farmers can track irrigation needs remotely.

Temperature & Humidity

DHT22 provides accurate ambient readings. Essential for monitoring frost risk, heat stress, and optimal growing conditions.

MQ Gas Sensor

Detects methane, ammonia, and other gases relevant to soil health. Abnormal readings are flagged and sent to the farmer's dashboard.

Emergency Alert System

Admin-issued alerts reach all registered users within seconds — SMS, email, and in-app notifications

Critical

Disease Outbreaks

Immediate alerts when new crop diseases are identified in your region. Includes symptoms to watch for, treatment recommendations, and affected crop types.

Early warnings about pest outbreaks like fall armyworm or locust swarms. Provides prevention measures and recommended pesticide usage with safety guidelines.

How AgroPan Works

Four steps from sign-up to smarter farming and trading.

Create Your Account

Sign up as a Farmer or Merchant. Your account unlocks the full platform — marketplace, forum, alerts, and IoT dashboard access.

Connect Your Field Device

Deploy the ESP32 sensor unit in your field. It automatically sends soil, temperature, humidity, and gas data to your AgroPan dashboard.

Trade on the Marketplace

Farmers list produce with pricing. Merchants browse and contact sellers directly. Track market trends and make informed trade decisions.

Grow Together

Join the community forum, receive emergency alerts, and build lasting connections. Better information means better farming outcomes.
